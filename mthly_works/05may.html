Prepare pits (50 x 50 x 50 cm) on northern side of the standards, 15-30 cm away from base of the standard. Fill the pits with mixture of topsoil and FYM or compost @ 5 kg/pit mixed with biocontrol agents VIZ. , T. harzianum (50 g/pit) and P. chlamydosporia (50 g/pit). 

Tie and trail the growing shoots to the standards. 

Provide shade to the plants, if the land is exposed. Shade can be removed after receiving I or2 showers. 

Remove and destroy all infected or dead vines along with the root system. 

Prune the runner shoots or tie back to the standards before the onset of monsoon. 

Irrigate the plants at regular intervals to maintain low temperature and high humidity depending on water avai lability. 

Prune the branches of standards, if pruning was not done during April.
