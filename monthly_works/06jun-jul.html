ಸಾಗುವಳಿ ಕೆಲಸಗಳು
ನೀರು ನಿಲ್ಲದ ಹಾಗೆ ಡ್ರೈನೇಜ್ ವ್ಯವಸ್ಥೆ ಮಾಡಿ 
ಎಳೆಯ ಬಳ್ಳಿಗಳ ಬೆಳೆಯುತ್ತಿರುವ ಚಿಗುರುಗಳನ್ನು ಮರಕ್ಕೆ ಕಟ್ಟಿ
Plant 2-3 rooted cuttings in the pits at a distance of about 30 cm away from the standards after receiving sufficient rain
Press the soil around the cuttings to form a small mound slopping outwards and away from the cuttings to prevent water stagnation around the plants
Undertake frequent inspection of recently planted vines for pest/disease infestations and take necessary protection measures
Undertake slash weeding in the interspaces
 
ಗೊಬ್ಬರದ ನಿರ್ವಹಣೆ
ಮಣ್ಣಿನ PH ಅಂಶ 5ಕ್ಕಿಂತ ಕಡಿಮೆ ಇದ್ದಲ್ಲಿ, 1000g ಡೋಲೋಮೈಟ್ ಅನ್ನು ಪ್ರತಿ ಬಳ್ಳಿಗೆ ಹಾಕಬೇಕು  
Apply organic manures @ 10 kg/vine and neem cake @ 1 kg/mature vine fortified with Tricoderma and Pochonia with the receipt of monsoon showers (vines more than 3 years of age).
Apply Azospirillum (50 g/vine).
Apply half the quantity/dose of (NPK50:50:150 g/vine/year as general recommendation i.e Urea 55 g: Rock Phosphate 140 g: muriate of potash 125g) per plant of more than 3 years old and care should be taken to avoid injuries to the vines/roots while applying fertilizers and other intercultural operations
Apply 1 kg neem cake, 200 g rock phosphate, 0.5 kg ash and 10 kg cow dung per vine, if organic system is 6 AugustSeptember fo llowed. Apply 150 g sulphate of potash, if potassium deficiency persists in soil
Spray black pepper micronutrient mixture @ 5 gm per liter water
Apply first round of biocontrol agents (Trichoderma and Pochollia chlamydosporia) along with organic matter/manures
 
ರೋಗ ನಿಯಂತ್ರಣ
Undertake foliar spray with Bordeaux mixture 1% followed by soil drenching over a radius of 45-50 cm around the vines with copper oxychloride (0.2%) (@ 2-5 litres per vine), immediately after first monsoon showers, if Phytophthora incidence is noticed
Drench the soil and spray foliage with potassium phosphonate (0.3%) or metalaxyl -mancozeb (0.125%)(@ 2-5 litres per vine)
Undertake foliar spray with quinalphos (0.05%) to check pollu beetle or top shoot borer. Leaf gall thrips can be controlled by spraying dimethoate (0.05%)
Avoid drenching of chemical pesticides, if biocontrol agents are applied.
