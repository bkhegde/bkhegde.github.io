April works

Plant cuttings of Murikku, Karayam, Kilingil or Glyricidia of2 meter length at a spacing 3 x 3 meter with the receipt of summer showers  

Irrigate the plants at regular intervals to maintain low temperature and high humidity depending on availability of water. .

Apply dolomite @500 g per vine after receiving rain if not applied during previous month 

Spray micronutrient mixture for vigorous growth

Regulate shade by pruning branches of standards (support trees)
