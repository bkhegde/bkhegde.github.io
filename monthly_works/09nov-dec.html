Works to be undertanken in Nov-Dec 

Tie the growing shoots of young vines to the standards. 

Young vines to be covered with dry arecanut or coconut leaves/fronds. 

Mulch the basin s with green leaves@lOkgperplant. 

Remove hanging shoots from older plants. 

Destroy plants showing symptoms of phyllody and virus diseases. 

In case of root mealy bugs infestation, drench the affected vines with chlorpyriphos (0.075%) and repeat drenching after 21 days. 

In case of scale insect infestation, spray neem oil (0.3%) or neem based insecticides (0.3%) or dimethoate (0.1 %) and repeat the sprays after 15 days.
